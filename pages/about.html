About

Kalimbas, also known as thumb pianos, are traditional African instruments with metal tines mounted on a wooden resonator. They produce a warm, melodic sound ideal for both relaxation and performance. Available in various sizes and tunings, kalimbas are cherished for their versatility and ease of play.
